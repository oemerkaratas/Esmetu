Crafted With Care

From Our Kitchen to Your Event

Esmetu Catering began the way all good meals do — around a family table. We bring generations of Mediterranean and Turkish cooking to your celebrations: recipes passed down, perfected, and made from scratch every single time.

Every menu starts with the season's best. Charcoal-grilled meats, garden-fresh mezze, and bread baked the morning of your event. No shortcuts, no pre-made trays — just honest food, made with care.

From intimate dinners to five-hundred-guest weddings, we cook like you're family. Because at your table, you are.

Our Story

Esmetu started in Esma's kitchen — a place where no one ever left hungry and every dish carried a story. What began as cooking for neighbors and family celebrations grew, plate by plate, into the catering house we are today.

We're still family-run. Still rolling sarma by hand, still baking every morning, still arguing about whose baklava is best. The recipes come from Gaziantep and the Aegean coast; the welcome comes from the heart.

When we cater your event, we bring more than food — we bring the table culture we grew up with: generous, unhurried, and made for sharing.

From our family to yours. — The Esmetu Family
